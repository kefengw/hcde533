A1: Laser Cut and 3D modeling

by Kefeng Wang in HCDE598

This is the final outcome of my work: Burj Khalifa in the Mill.

There are 16 pieces of each type. The longer strips are 3.93 inches long(10cm) and 0.78 inches long (2cm).
And the round pieces are 2.48 inches in radius. Each component has 4 notches. The notches are 3mm in
width and 6mm in depth. They could help to connect pieces together.

I measured the thickness of the cardboard I would use to cut. That is the reason why I specially set
the width of notches to 3 mm.

Based on the size of this cardboard, I have created the layout for my print plan in AI.

I borrowed laptops from the Mill and used Adobe AI for printing. It was really difficult at first when I was
trying to change the starting point to the concern of my cardboard.
